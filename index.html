Nail Trim
Walk-in

Long nails are your pet's enemy. They can cling and snag or break on things, they can lead to foot pain and the bending of their toes into uncomfortable directions. Keeping nails at a healthy, but short length helps your pet be the best version of themselves :) This service is automatically included in any of our bath or grooming packages, but if your pet can't wait until next groom visit, please don't hesitate to stop by for this walk-in service!

Dematt And
Brush Out

15 min. brush out is included with your bathing/grooming packages. Additional time needed or desired to brush out pet's coat or dematt will be cbarged in 15 min. increments. This is a great walk-in service as well for those gorgeous double coated pets: shepards, corgis, huskies, etc. Bring pet in for a 15 min. coat blow out service and you watch the loose undercoat blow right out like snow and help reduce shedding around the house:)

Specialized
Shampoo

We have a variety of hypo-allergenic shampoos made for sensitive skin, brightening white coats, bringing that extra shine to black or dark coats and we also have a flea and tick shampoo for when those pesky little buggers come knocking! Available as add-on service. In case of fleas, it's an automatic upgrade, we want to make sure your pet goes home happy, clean and pest free! Make sure to treat home bedding, carpets and toys to keep fleas from returning :)
